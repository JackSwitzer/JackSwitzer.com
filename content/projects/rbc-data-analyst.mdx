# Data Analyst at Royal Bank of Canada

My 2023 internship at RBC as a Data Analyst focused on automation, time series forecasting, and knowledge sharing across the team.

## Key Achievements

### Executive Dashboard Automation
Automated the weekly visualization of key business metrics for executives using Python. This automation:
- **Saved 8 hours weekly** of manual report generation
- Ensured consistent, error-free reporting
- Freed up team capacity for higher-value analysis work

### Call Centre Volume Forecasting
Conducted time series analysis to forecast call centre volume using SARIMA (Seasonal AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average) models:
- Achieved **less than 5% error** on yearly forecasting model
- Enabled better staffing decisions and resource allocation
- Provided reliable predictions for capacity planning

### Internal Training Program
Designed and taught a Python course with applications to forecasting for a team of twelve colleagues:
- Covered Python fundamentals and data manipulation
- Focused on practical forecasting applications
- Enabled team members to contribute to technical projects

## Skills Developed

This role strengthened my abilities in:
- Building automated data pipelines
- Statistical modeling and forecasting
- Technical communication and teaching
- Delivering business value through data analysis
