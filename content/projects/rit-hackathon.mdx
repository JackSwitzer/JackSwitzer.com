# RIT Hackathon - Market Making

A quantitative finance hackathon project focused on market making strategies and algorithmic trading.

## Trading Strategies

### Market Making
Implemented market making strategies based on:
- **Mean-Reversion**: Exploiting price deviations from historical averages
- **Risk Analysis**: Managing exposure and position sizing

### Liability Trading
Developed algorithms for liability trading, managing risk exposure while capturing market opportunities.

## Competition Context

Built for the Rotman International Trading Competition, one of the premier university trading competitions that simulates real market conditions.

## Technical Implementation

Developed in Python with focus on:
- Real-time decision making
- Order management
- Risk controls
- Performance analytics
